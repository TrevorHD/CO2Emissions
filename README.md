# Overview

A brief case study analysing and visualising estimated CO2 emissions across the world from 1994 to 2014. Here we focus on the top 15 CO2-emitting countries, comparing each country's annual and total estimated emissions to those of their peers, while also comparing the top 15 coutries as a whole to the rest of the world.

<br/>

# Files

## Data

**CO2_Data** *(.csv)* - Spreadsheet with data on estimated annual carbon dioxide emissions (in kilotonnes) by country.

## Figures

**CO2_Plots** *(.jpeg)* - Plots of the top 15 countries by estimated total CO2 emissions from 1994-2014, as well as how their estimated annual emissions change over time.

## Reports

**CO2_Report** *(.pdf)* - Report for the case study.

## Scripts

**CO2_Script_1** *(.R)* - Script for conducting analyses and generating figures.

**CO2_Script_2** *(.Rmd)* - Script for rendering output to PDF.
