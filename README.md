# Overview

A brief case study analysing and visualising estimated CO2 emissions across the world over the course of two decades (1994-2014). Here we focus on the top 15 CO2-emitting countries, comparing each country's annual and total emissions to those of their peers, while also comparing the top 15 coutries as a whole to the rest of the world.

<br/>

# Files

## Data

**CO2Data** *(.csv)* - Spreadsheet containing World Bank data on estimated annual carbon dioxide emissions (in kilotonnes) by country.

## Figures

**CO2Plots** *(.jpeg)* - Plots showing the top 15 countries by estimated total CO2 emissions from 1994-2014, as well as how their estimated annual emissions change over time.

## Scripts

**CO2_1** *(.R)* - Script for conducting most of the data analyses.

**CO2_2** *(.Rmd)* - Script for rendering output to PDF.

## Writeups

**CO2Emissions** *(.pdf)* - Report for the case study.
